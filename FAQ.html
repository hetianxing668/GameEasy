# Q: What is pending e-check, How do I avoid sending an e-check?

# A:A pending-e-check, like writing an electronically check, is a kind of payment method for Pay Pal. It will take days to clear as Pay Pal needs to verify the funds within the senders account. Once the check is cleared, the payment will be transferred to our account and your order is approved immediately.

An unverified account without sufficient balance of your Paypal account results in an e-check. And it usually takes 3-4 days for the payment provider to contact your bank to verify that funds. So if you want to avoid an e-check, the verification process for your account must be completed and there are sufficient funds available.
